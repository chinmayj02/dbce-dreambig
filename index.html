Join us for the third edition of DBCE Dream Big, an Industry Student Connect event hosted by the Computer Engineering Department on March 15th, 2024 . Our aim is to foster awareness about innovation in industrial practices and professional expertise among students.
This immersive event serves as an invaluable experiential learning opportunity, allowing students to engage directly with industry experts. Through interactive sessions, participants will gain insights into industry work patterns and the latest technologies, transcending traditional classroom learning.
By facilitating direct interaction with seasoned professionals, we aspire to equip young minds with real-world knowledge and practical insights beyond the confines of textbooks. This unique experience not only enriches students' learning journey but also nurtures invaluable industry connections right here at the institute level.
Don't miss this chance to broaden your horizons and set the stage for your future career success. Join us at DBCE Dream Big and embark on a journey of discovery and professional growth..

Our Speakers

Sri Charan Lakkaraju

Founder & CEO, Student Tribe, Hyderabad

ABOUT

Sri Charan Lakkaraju, the visionary Founder & CEO behind Student Tribe, leads a groundbreaking student discovery platform revolutionizing opportunities for students from tier 2 and tier 3 colleges. With a rich history dating back to 2015, Sri Charan initiated this transformative venture as stuMagz, a pioneering student community facilitating internship discoveries, opportunities, and student events. His exceptional leadership has earned him recognition as a Forbes 30Under30 Asia 2018 honoree, alongside his esteemed position as a Charter Member at TiE Hyderabad, a global nexus of entrepreneurs and investors. Driven by an unwavering passion for empowering students, Sri Charan has impacted over 300,000 individuals through comprehensive training encompassing Entrepreneurship, soft skills, interview techniques, and recruitment tests.

Vishal Desai

CEO and Co-Founder, The Grit City, Goa

ABOUT

Vishal Desai, a member of The Grit City team, is dedicated to shaping the future of higher education with self-directed, experiential, and design-led approaches. With over 20 years of experience in consumer tech product development, his expertise includes design research, user-centered design, lean methodologies, and product-led growth. Passionate about using design thinking to drive innovation, Vishal offers coaching and conducts workshops on entrepreneurship, LinkedIn, and product management. As a featured speaker at various organizations and Higher Education institutions, including Product School and Goa Engineering College, he inspires audiences to embrace design thinking for impactful solutions. Vishal welcomes connections with investors and thought leaders interested in education innovation and human-centered design to collaborate on projects that prepare young talent for success in the 21st Century.

Srinivas Taluka

VP Facilities, T- HUB, Hyderabad

ABOUT

Srinivas Taluka, a seasoned management professional with an illustrious 25-year career, epitomizes excellence in Facility Management, Infrastructure Management, General Administration, Marketing, and Corporate Communication. With a rich portfolio spanning diverse sectors including IT, Pharma, BPO, and manufacturing, Srinivas has honed his expertise in facilities management at esteemed organizations such as GE Capital, GVKBIO, CSC, and SIGNODE. Currently serving as a pivotal force at T-Hub, Srinivas plays a vital role in nurturing the startup ecosystem, fueling innovation and entrepreneurship. His ambition is to elevate T-Hub to the gold standard for innovation worldwide. Srinivas's remarkable capabilities encompass a broad spectrum of specialties, including setting up new facilities, managing multiple facilities, leading large teams, and ensuring robust administrative support. He is hailed for his proficiency in crisis management, vendor contract management, and establishing standards and protocols.

Damodar Pai Patnekar

CEO and Co-Founder, Bodhami

ABOUT

Damodar Pai Patnekar, a National Startup Award winner recognized by the Government of India, is the dynamic Co-founder and CEO of Bodhami, an innovative edutech startup. Expand With a focus on career guidance and skill development through Artificial Intelligence, Damodar has garnered acclaim for his contributions to youth, women, and rural development, earning the prestigious Goan Achievers Award from the Government of Goa. An MBA gold medalist and computer engineering graduate, Damodar's expertise extends over 22 years in information technology and services. His leadership at Bodhami has led to multiple accolades, including national awards and recognition at global events like the Vivatech Startup Festival in Paris. Specializing in digital and automation solutions for insurers, Damodar drives transformative initiatives and agile transformations for Fortune 500 companies.
